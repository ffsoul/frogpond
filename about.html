Hey there!
I'm Kenny Ly!
I'm a graphic designer and illustrator who has recently graduated from The University of Texas at Austin with a Bachelor's in Fine Arts in Design.
Over the past few years, I've had an avid interest in designing with print, having the opportunity to work with the Rob Roy Kelly American Wood Type Collection as both a summer resident and a design lab technician. I've also spent my time at UT researching interaction and community design as part of my independent studies. My personal work involves managing online collaborative art book projects, as well as offering digital art commissions.
My core beliefs as a designer are to make designs that work to support communities and are straight to the point, all while adding in my own "injection of weirdness". In my free time, I spend my time gaming, drawing, or picking up a random new skill to learn for a few weeks.
