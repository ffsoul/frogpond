About Me
Hey there!
I'm Kenny Ly!
I'm Kenny, a graphic and interaction designer in my fourth year of pursuing a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Design at UT Austin.
Over the past few years, I've had an avid interest in designing with print, having the opportunity to work with the Rob Roy Kelly American Wood Type Collection during my summer residency and for my personal project exhibition. I've also spent my time at UT researching interaction and community design as part of my independent study.
My core beliefs as a designer are to make designs that work to support communities and are straight to the point, all while adding in my own "injection of weirdness". In my free time, I spend my time gaming, drawing, or picking up a random new skill to learn for a few weeks.
