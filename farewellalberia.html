project overview
After the annoucement of end of service for the game Dragalia Lost in 2022, members of the online community gathered together to create a fanwork to celebrate life of the game. Over 100 artists, writers, and voice actors came together to create this zine, led by the Farewell Alberia Team. I was tasked with creating the branding materials, social media posts, and helping with final page layouts.
Disclaimer: Various characters and artwork are properties of Cygames Inc. and are used only for non-profit transformative works.
