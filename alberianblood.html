Alberian Blood

typography, art, design
project overview
This assignment in Typography II tasked me with creating a zine involving parataxis, using different printing techniques, and organizing a group of guest artists to create a commemorative fan artbook for the game "Dragalia Lost". Each page needed to include some form of typography and the zine was required to have instances of digital and physical manipulation of type.
