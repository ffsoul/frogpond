typography, art, design
project overview
This assignment in Typography II tasked me with creating a zine involving parataxis, using different printing techniques, and organizing a group of guest artists to create a commemorative work for the game "Dragalia Lost". Each page needed to include some form of typography and the zine was required to have instances of digital and physical manipulation of type.
Disclaimer: Various characters and artwork are properties of Cygames Inc. and are used only for non-profit transformative works.
