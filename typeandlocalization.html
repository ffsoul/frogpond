Typography and Localization

Typography, Art, Design
project overview
How does typography play into some of our own personal interests? This project explored the typography behind the logos of the game Dragalia Lost, a game I hold close to my heart, and analyzes the structure of a “gacha game” logo and how their design works differently due to linguistic differences.
Disclaimer: Various characters and artwork are properties of Cygames Inc. and are used only for non-profit transformative works.
